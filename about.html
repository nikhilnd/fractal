This fractal is based of of L-Systems, or Lindenmayer System, named after Hungarian biologist Arstid Lindenmayer. At its base, an L-System is a simple but elegant recursive text based model. It consists of a starting vaiable or axiom, a rule(s), and an alphabet. For example, your starting axiom might be "A" with the rule A becomes AB and B becomes A. So after one iteration, the single A becomes AB. After the second iteration, it will be ABA. However, these systems can also be represented visually and consist of multiple variables, rules, and constants. In this L-System, the starting axiom is X which is represented by a line of arbitrary length. The rule is X becomes F+[[X]-X]-F[-FX]+X and F becomes FF in which F draws a line with half the length of X. "+" rotates by 40 degrees. "-" rotates by -40 degrees. X draws a line of half last line length. "[" means to save the current drawing state, and "]" means to reset the drawing state.

Source code is available at: https://github.com/nikhilnd/fractal
